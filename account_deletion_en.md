# Account & Data Deletion Request (English)

**"The King's Way"** respects your privacy and data sovereignty. In compliance with Google Play Store's Data Safety policies, we provide a clear path for you to request the deletion of your account and all associated data.

## 1. Data Deleted
Upon processing an account deletion request, the following data points are permanently removed from our systems:
- **Account Credentials**: Email address, unique User ID, and third-party authentication tokens (Google).
- **Game Progress**: Daily progress (Day count), history of read books, and unlocked achievements (Royal Ranks).
- **Activity Logs**: Completed Jo-gang sessions, Royal Ratification decisions, and governance logs.
- **Preferences**: In-app custom settings (font size, audio volume, language preferences).

## 2. How to Request Deletion

### Method A: In-App Direct Deletion (Recommended)
This is the fastest and most secure method if you still have the app installed.
1. Launch **The King's Way** and navigate to the **Library (Main Screen)**.
2. Tap the **Settings (Gear Icon)** in the top right corner.
3. Scroll to the bottom and tap **'Withdraw Account' (계정 탈퇴)**.
4. Read the warning carefully and tap **'Confirm Withdrawal'**. Your data will be deleted instantly and you will be signed out.

### Method B: Web / Email Request
If you have already uninstalled the app or cannot access it, use the following methods:
- **GitHub Issue**: Create a new issue in this repository ([Issues](https://github.com/ChaPDCha/the-kingsway-privacy-policy/issues)) with the title "Account Deletion Request". (For your privacy, *do not* post your email address publicly).
- **Developer Email**: Contact us at the developer email address listed in the App Store profile with your registered email address. We will verify your identity and process the request within 7 business days.

## 3. Data Retention Policy
- We prioritize immediate destruction of user data upon request.
- Exception: Certain metadata required by law (e.g., transaction records if applicable) may be retained for the minimum period required by local regulations before final disposal.
- Warning: Deleted data **cannot** be recovered.

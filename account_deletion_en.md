# Account & Data Deletion Request

**\"The King's Way\"** respects your privacy rights and data sovereignty. In accordance with the Google Play Store's Data Safety policies and relevant regulations, we provide the following instructions for account and data deletion.

## 1. Scope of Data Deletion
Upon requesting account deletion (withdrawal), the following data will be subject to deletion:
- **Account Information**: Email address, social login identifiers, nickname.
- **Progress Data**: Current day (Day count), book reading history, achievements, and royal ranks.
- **Activity Logs**: Completed Jo-gang sessions, Royal Meeting decisions, and in-game resources (Vitality, etc.).
- **Preferences**: Font size, background/effect volume, push notification settings, etc.

## 2. How to Request Account Deletion

### Method A: In-App Request (Recommended)
This is the easiest and most secure way to process deletion while logged in.
1. Navigate to the **Settings (Gear Icon)** menu from the bottom or main screen.
2. Click the **'Withdraw Account' (계정 탈퇴)** menu at the bottom.
3. Review the instructions and select **'Withdraw'** to complete the request.

### Method B: Request via Web/Email
If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion through the following official channels:
- **Email Inquiries**: Please send a request including your registered account information to [chapdcha@gmail.com](mailto:chapdcha@gmail.com).
- **GitHub Issue**: You can also request via the [Issues](https://github.com/ChaPDCha/the-kingsway-privacy-policy/issues) tab of this repository. (Please be careful not to post your full email address in a public space.)

## 3. Grace Period (15 Days)
To prevent data loss due to user error, **'The King's Way' maintains a 15-day grace period for deletion** from the date of the request.
- During the grace period, your data will be kept in an inactive state on the server, and you can withdraw the deletion request at any time by logging in again.
- **After the grace period (15 days) has passed, all data will be permanently destroyed and cannot be recovered under any circumstances.**

## 4. Data Retention Policy (Exceptions)
Information that must be retained for a certain period even after withdrawal in accordance with relevant laws is as follows:
- **Website Visit Records**: 3 months (Communication Secrets Protection Act)
- **Records on Consumer Complaints or Dispute Resolution**: 3 years (Electronic Commerce Act)
- **Abuse Records**: To prevent re-registration and enforce service policies, minimum identifiers (hashed values) may be kept for 6 months.
